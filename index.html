welcome to pig dice

Game Rules

The Game is to score 100 points before the opponents do(s).The game is played with a single six-sided dice.

The Game

In each turn a player rolls the dice repeatedly untill either "1" is rolled or player decides to hold and pass on the turn to its opponent.

The number player rolls is added to a temporary score; If the player rolls a 1, he lose his temporary score and turn is passed to their opponent.

If the player gets any other number he can decide to continue and roll the dice.

If the player decides to pass, then his turn total is added to the overall score.

The first player to score 100 points wins the game.
